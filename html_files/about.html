SilverCare Initiative is a community-driven, non-profit organization based in North Vancouver dedicated to improving the health, comfort, and independence of seniors. We provide residential care, nursing services, and daily living assistance for adults aged 60 and above, ensuring that every resident feels safe, respected, and connected.

Our facilities are designed to be accessible, friendly, and supportive, offering not only professional healthcare but also a vibrant social environment. Residents can enjoy group activities, wellness programs, cultural celebrations, and personalized care plans that cater to their unique needs.

At SilverCare, our mission is to enhance the quality of life for seniors through compassionate care, holistic health support, and meaningful community engagement.
