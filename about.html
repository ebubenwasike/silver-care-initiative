Our Mission & Vision

SilverCare Initiative is a community-driven, non-profit organization based in North Vancouver, dedicated to improving the health, comfort, and independence of seniors aged 60 and above.

We believe that aging should be a time of dignity, connection, and fulfillment. Our mission is to provide exceptional residential care, nursing services, and daily living assistance while fostering a vibrant, supportive community environment.
